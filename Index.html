About us

River Kwai is Victoria’s leading professional caterer for corporate events, weddings, office functions, social occasions and celebrations.

River Kwai restaurant is open 7 nights a week from 5:30pm-10pm River Kwai is famous for the ‘bridge’ that to us, represents the joining of two beautiful countries; Thailand and Burma (Myanmar).We strive for authenticity, large portions, friendly service, a great wine list and beautiful decor.

Vegetarian

A brief introduction to vegetarianism


River Kwai is well known for having an extensive range of vegetarian options.Our vegetarian food is 100% vegetarian. No Shrimp paste is used in our curry pastes; fish sauce is also not used in any of our vegetarian dishes.We use fresh market Vegetables mixed with Tofu and egg is optional.Our Vegetarian curry puffs and spring rolls are hand made so we can assure you of the 100% vegetarian policy we have.Try the chili basil tofu and mixed vegetables, a common favourite.
